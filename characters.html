-Characters-





Anderson

Anderson is a 25-year-old man from Navia. At first glance, he just seems like an impeccably clad, spoiled socialite, but he’s knowledgeable in a vast number of subjects, especially the sciences. He’s the only child, so spent a lot of time hanging out with the main character while residing in their hometown, Navia. He’s very close to his uncle, Byron, because he was a very influential and positive male role model for Anderson growing up.






Gia

A 27-year-old woman, who escaped her cultish hometown, Albion. She’s a very passionate woman who knows the value of hard work. She works at the northern Galleon library and loves to read and write literature. Gia is idealistic and sometimes makes poor decisions that get her into trouble. She left her family when she was 16, and eventually ended up in Galleon. Despite not wanting to go back to her hometown, she really misses her baby sister, Mara.
